## Commit Messages
- 1行目 (subject): 英語。imperative form (e.g. `Add commit message rules to templates`)
- 2行目: 空行
- 3行目以降 (body): 日本語可。変更理由・詳細を記述
- 本文は必要に応じて箇条書きにしてよい

## Temporary Files
- Put temporary work under `tmp/<purpose>/`.
- Do not create root-level `tmp_*` or `tmp-*` directories.
- Remove temporary directories when they are no longer needed.
